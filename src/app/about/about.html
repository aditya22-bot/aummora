About Aumorra International

Bringing India’s herbal, wellness & natural products to the world — with purity, integrity & trust.

Aumorra International is a fast-growing merchant-exporter from India, delivering premium-quality herbal powders, spices, oils, nutraceutical ingredients, and FMCG products to global and domestic markets. Built on strong farmer relationships and a commitment to authentic Indian agriculture, we ensure consistent quality, seamless compliance, and on-time delivery for every order.

Our Story

Aumorra International began with a simple ambition — to showcase India’s rich herbal heritage and natural resources to the world. With strong roots in agriculture and an understanding of global product demands, we started our journey as a small export and ecommerce-driven company.

Today, Aumorra International has expanded into a multi-category, export-focused brand serving clients across Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and Africa. While we’ve grown, our core belief remains unchanged: quality first, relationships forever, and transparency at every step.

Our Vision

To become a globally trusted partner for ethically-sourced herbal, wellness, and natural FMCG products — representing India’s quality on the global stage.

Our Mission

  • Enable global businesses to access pure, consistent, and responsibly sourced ingredients from India.
  • Strengthen farmer communities through fair pricing and long-term partnerships.
  • Build a supply chain where quality, traceability, and compliance come standard.
